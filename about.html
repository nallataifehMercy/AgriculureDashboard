Empowering Agricultural Insight through Satellite Technology

Welcome to the Syria Agriculture Monitoring Platform, an open-access tool developed to support agricultural monitoring and planning across Syrian governorates, districts, and subdistricts. Our platform uses Google Earth Engine (GEE) Platform, and advanced machine learning techniques to provide accurate, temporal, and spatial information about agriculural domain in Syria.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support agricultural development and food security in Syria through data-driven insights and monitoring tools. By providing accurate and timely information, we aim to help farmers, aid organizations, and policymakers make informed decisions about agricultural practices and resource allocation.

Key Features

  • Water Harvesting: Monitor water resources and harvesting potential across different regions
  • Agriculture Monitoring: Track crop health and agricultural conditions using satellite imagery
  • Crop Identification: Identify crop types and distribution patterns

Technology

This dashboard utilizes advanced technologies including satellite imagery analysis, machine learning for crop identification, and geospatial data processing. The platform is built using modern web technologies to ensure accessibility and ease of use.

Partners

This project is a collaborative effort between multiple organizations committed to supporting agricultural development in Syria.
